# Onigiri Escape 🍙🏃‍♂️

An Unreal Engine side-scroller developed as an **international high school collaboration** between Sweden and Japan. This project focused on cross-cultural cooperation, game design, and international networking.

## 🌟 The Story
You play as a sentient **Onigiri** trapped in a kitchen environment. Your mission: platform your way through obstacles to escape to the freedom of the outside world!

## ✨ Features
*   **Unique Character Design**: Play as a custom-modeled Onigiri with physics-based movement.
*   **International Collaboration**: Co-designed and developed in partnership with students from a **Japanese High School**.
*   **Side-Scrolling Action**: Classic 2.5D platforming mechanics built in Unreal Engine.
*   **Environmental Storytelling**: Navigate from the indoor kitchen "prison" to the outdoor world.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
*   **Engine**: Unreal Engine (UE4/UE5)
*   **Scripting**: Blueprints
*   **Collaboration Tools**: Discord & Git (used for cross-border version control)
